Actual Energy minization steps:



Step2: Generate topology
gmx pdb2gmx -f THAP1Lonchura09_03_1b67d_relaxed_rank_001_alphafold2_ptm_model_4_seed_000.pdb  -ignh
-> 8(charmm27) -> 1(TIP3P)


3. Define the simulation box
gmx editconf -f conf.gro  -d 1.0 -bt triclinic -o box.gro


4. Solvate the system
gmx solvate -cp box.gro -cs spc216.gro -p topol.top -o box_sol.gro


5. Prepare system for ion addition
gmx grompp -f ions.mdp -c box_sol.gro -p topol.top -o ION.tpr


6. Neutralize the system
gmx genion -s ION.tpr -p topol.top -conc 0.1 -neutral -o box_sol_ion.gro
-> 13 (SOL)


7. Prepare energy minimization
gmx grompp -f EM.mdp -c box_sol_ion.gro -p topol.top -o EM.tpr


8. Run energy minimization
gmx mdrun -v -deffnm EM


9. Final PDB conversion 
gmx editconf -f EM.gro -o EM_minimized.pdb


10. Saving PDB only without water molecules and ions -> Because after energy minization the size of the pdb increases due to those water and ion molecules -> And large sized PDB files are not accepted on those docking webserver.
gmx trjconv -s EM.tpr -f EM.gro -n index.ndx -o protein_only.pdb
-> 1 (select protein)
